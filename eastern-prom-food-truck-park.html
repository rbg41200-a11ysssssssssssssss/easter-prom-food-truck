Portland's Outdoor Dining Gem

Sea Air,
Great Food

Nestled along the scenic Eastern Promenade overlooking Casco Bay, Eastern Prom Food Truck Park is Portland's most beloved casual dining destination.

A rotating lineup of Portland's best local food trucks set up daily — from Mr. Tuna's legendary hand rolls and sushi to Falafel Mafia's crispy falafels, Vy Bánh Mì's Cajun lobster bánh mì, Cargo Pizza's wood-fired pies, and Gelato Fiasco's unforgettable scoops.

Grab your food, find a picnic table or a spot on the grass, and watch the sailboats drift across the bay. Dogs are welcome, kids will love it, and the vibe is always good.
